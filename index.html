Welcome

Learn setup, turn actions, pipelines, duels, and item capture step by step.

Step 1: Game Setup

  • Select Avatar
  • Place Item(s) behind Avatar
  • Draw 3 cards (Player 1 gets +1)

Step 2: Turn Actions

  • Place Limb connecting to Avatar or pipeline
  • Draw 1 card (hand max 5)
  • Rotate Limb 180° (counts as action)
  • Bonus action if win a duel

Step 3: Pipelines

Each Avatar has two pipelines. Chains must be continuous and not branch.

Step 4: Dueling

Collisions trigger R/P/S duels. Max 3 exchanges. Winner gains bonus action.

Step 5: Item Capture

Reach an Item, duel, capture to win the round.
